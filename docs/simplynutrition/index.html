Meet The Owners

Meet The People Behind
Simply Nutrition

Simply Nutrition was born right here in Wheelersburg - not from a corporate playbook, but from a genuine passion for healthy living and a love for this community. We saw what The Burg needed and we showed up.

We're your neighbors. We opened our doors on Ohio River Rd because Wheelersburg is home, and we wanted to give this town something it could be proud of - a place where healthy drinks taste incredible and everyone is welcome.

Locally owned and operated right here in The Burg
Built on a passion for health, flavor, and community
Clean energy - no jitters, no crash, just results
