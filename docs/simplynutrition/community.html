Game Day & Sports Events

From football season to championship runs, Simply Nutrition fuels The Burg's athletes. We show up to pregame events, provide energy drinks for teams, and cheer louder than anyone. The road to Canton starts with great nutrition.

Fundraisers & Giving Back

We regularly partner with local organizations and causes to raise funds and awareness. A portion of every special event sale goes back to the community that built us. We believe good business means doing good - period.

Gym Partnerships

We work directly with local gyms and fitness coaches to help athletes fuel their training the right way. Our protein shakes and recovery bowls are designed with active people in mind - and our local gym partners back that up every day.

Performance Drinks
Protein and recovery options designed specifically for athletes and active lifestyles.
Workout Fuel
Pre and post-workout nutrition that supports real training goals and muscle recovery.
Partner Discounts
Local gym members receive exclusive discounts. Ask us about our partnership program.

School Support

The future of Wheelersburg is its young people - and we invest in them. Simply Nutrition supports local school fundraisers, athletic programs, and student organizations. When the next generation thrives, our whole community wins.
