Our Mission & Story

Simply Nutrition was built on a simple belief - that healthy, energizing drinks should taste incredible, be affordable, and be right here in your community. We started because we saw a gap, and we filled it with love.

We serve the Wheelersburg community with pride every single day. Whether you're a gym-goer looking for the perfect post-workout shake, someone wanting a healthier alternative to your morning coffee, or just a person who wants to feel good, we have a drink made for you.

Our goal is simple: help you feel energized, healthy, and ready to take on your day. Every ingredient, every flavor, every cup we make is crafted with that mission in mind. We're not just a nutrition shop - we're part of your routine, your lifestyle, and your community.
