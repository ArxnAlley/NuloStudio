Our Story

Built on hard work and trusted throughout the Ohio Valley.

Ohio Valley Construction & Plumbing LLC was built from the ground up right here in Proctorville. We started as a small, hardworking crew that took pride in doing things the right way — and that hasn't changed one bit in over 20 years.

We're a locally owned and operated business, which means when you call us, you're talking to people who live in this community, know your neighbors, and genuinely care about the work we put into your home. No call centers. No subcontractors you've never met. Just skilled, dependable people who show up and get the job done.

Over the years, we've expanded from residential plumbing into full-service construction and home improvement — because our customers needed someone they could trust with more than just their pipes. Today, we handle everything from emergency plumbing at 2am to full home remodels and deck builds.
